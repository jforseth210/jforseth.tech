What is a parish code?

Having a large group of people praying for you is incredibly powerful. However, sometimes you only want to share your concerns with a small group of people you know and trust. A parish code is a word or phrase, like STJOHNRE, that you can use to send to a specific group of people, such as your youth group or church congregation. If you enter a parish code, that prayer request will only go to the people in that group. Don't know a parish code, or want to send your request to the entire prayer chain? Don't worry, you can just leave the field blank and the request will go to everyone.
